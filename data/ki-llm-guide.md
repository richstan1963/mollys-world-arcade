#### Basic Mechanics
"Killer Instinct" introduces a unique combo system with the following structure:
1. **Opener**: Start your combo with a basic attack.
2. **Auto-Double**: After an opener, if you press the same button twice, it becomes an auto-double.
3. **Linker**: Use a different type of attack (kick after punch or vice versa) to continue the combo.
4. **Auto-Double Again**: Repeat the second part of the first combo by pressing the same button twice again.
5. **Ender**: Finish your combo with a powerful move.

#### Combo Breakers
The "Combo Breaker" is perhaps one of the most memorable features. When an opponent uses their auto-double, you must defend using the same strength (light/medium/fierce) to break the combo. This adds a layer of strategy and excitement to every battle.
